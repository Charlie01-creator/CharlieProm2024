Our Prom Story

Central College Mityana's annual prom is a celebration of achievement, friendship, and the beginning of new journeys.

A Night to Remember

The 2024 prom was our most spectacular event yet, with over 300 students attending the gala evening at the Grand Imperial Ballroom. The black and gold theme created an atmosphere of elegance and sophistication that our students will remember for years to come.

From the red carpet arrivals to the crowning of prom royalty, every moment was captured by our team of professional photographers. We're proud to present these memories in stunning high-resolution quality.
