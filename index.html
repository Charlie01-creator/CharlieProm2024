About the Prom

Prom 2024 at Central College Mityana was a spectacular evening filled with joy, elegance, and unforgettable memories. From the glamorous red carpet entrance to the energetic dance floor, every moment was carefully crafted to celebrate our students' friendships, achievements, and bright futures ahead.
