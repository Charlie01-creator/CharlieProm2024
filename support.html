Frequently Asked Questions

How will my donation be used? +

Your donation will directly support prom expenses including venue decoration, catering, photography, entertainment, and awards. We maintain transparency and will share a post-event financial report with all donors.

Is there a minimum donation amount? +

There is no minimum donation amount. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us create a memorable experience for our students.

Will I receive a receipt for my donation? +

Yes, if you would like a receipt, please send a screenshot of your transaction confirmation to our finance office, and we will provide you with an official donation receipt.

Can I donate items instead of money? +

Yes, we welcome in-kind donations such as decorations, gifts for students, or services. Please contact us to discuss what you would like to contribute.

Need Help With Your Donation?

If you have any questions or need assistance with your donation, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
