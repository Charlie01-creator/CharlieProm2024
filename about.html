Our History

Founded in 1995, Central College Mityana has established itself as a premier institution of learning in the heart of Uganda. What began as a small community school has grown into a respected educational institution known for academic excellence and character development.

Over the decades, we have maintained our commitment to providing quality education that combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. Our alumni network includes leaders in various fields across Uganda and beyond.

The 2024 Prom represented another milestone in our rich history, celebrating the achievements of our students and creating lasting memories for the graduating class.
